We offer workshops and 1-1 bike mechanics training to teach everything from the basics of how to maintain your bike, to more specialized training on different components such as the different brake systems and wheel truing! To find and register for upcoming workshops, please click here. To request a workshop, please contact us!

Bike Donations

Got a new bike and think your old bike can be salvaged or used for parts. Please don't let it go to landfill. contact us, send a picture, and see if it has any potential. If it does, we would be happy to receive your donation which will help your community.

Mobile Service

Got a lot of bikes, not sure how to bring your bike to us, or just not feeling it? We offer mobile service - that means we will come to your residence and fix whatever your bike needs. Mobile service has additional charges based on your location. Please contact us us for details and to schedule mobile service for your bicycle!
